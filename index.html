Detection Methods

THE PIPELINE

👁️
01 — Eye Monitoring
Eye Aspect Ratio

Calculates EAR from six facial landmarks per eye. When the ratio drops below a calibrated threshold the system classifies eye-closure fatigue. Robust against glasses and variable lighting.

dlibMediaPipeNumPy
Accuracy94%
Deployed
😮
02 — Mouth Monitoring
Yawn Detection

Tracks MAR across 20 lip landmarks. Distinguishes yawns from speech through duration gating and ratio thresholding, logging frequency over a rolling 5-minute window.

dlibSciPyOpenCV
Accuracy91%
Deployed
🤦
03 — Postural Analysis
Head Pose Estimation

Estimates 3D head orientation via PnP solver on six anatomical points. Detects head drooping and micro-sleep by tracking pitch angle over time with staged alerts.

SolvePnPNumPyEuler Angles
Accuracy89%
Deployed
⚡
04 — Blink Analysis
Blink Rate Monitoring

Monitors blink frequency over a 60-second sliding window. Both abnormally high and low rates trigger alerts. Calibrates to each individual's baseline at session start.

MediaPipeDeque BufferKalman
Accuracy88%
Deployed
🧠
05 — Deep Learning
CNN Fatigue Classifier

CNN trained on labelled alert/fatigued facial states. Processes face regions at 24fps outputting a continuous fatigue probability. Augmented training for real-world variation.

TensorFlowKerasCNN
Accuracy93%
Deployed
🚨
06 — Fusion & Alerting
Multi-Modal Alert System

Fuses all five module signals via weighted voting into three alert tiers — Warning, Danger, Critical — with escalating audio/visual alerts and timestamped session logs.

PyGameWeighted FusionCSV
F1 Score92%
Deployed
